2. Feature Extraction¶
2.1. Feature Extraction from Time Domain¶
Moments of a signal: mean, variance, skewness, and kurtosis


    

  • mean
    • 


$$\bar x = \frac{1}{N} \sum_{n=1}^{N} x[n]$$
    

  • variance
    • 


$$\text{var} (x) = \frac{1}{N-1} \sum_{n=1}^{N} (x[n] - \bar x)^2$$$$\sigma = \sqrt{\text{var}}$$
    

  • Note: RMS (root mean square)
    • 


$$\text{RMS} = \sqrt{\frac{1}{N} \sum_{n=1}^{N}x[n]^2 } = \sqrt{\bar x ^2 + \sigma^2}$$






    

  • Skewness
      

    • a positive value of skewness signifies a distribution with an asymmetric tail extending out towards more positive $x$ 
      • 

    • a negative value signifies a distribution whose tail extends out towards more negative $x$
      • 

    

    • 


$$\text{skewness} (x) = \frac{1}{N} \sum_{n=1}^{N} \left(\frac{x[n] - \bar x}{\sigma} \right)^3$$



    

  • Kurtosis
      

    • It measures the relative peakedness or flatness of a distribution. Relative to what? A normal distribution
      • 

    

    • 


$$\text{kurtosis} (x) = \frac{1}{N} \sum_{n=1}^{N} \left(\frac{x[n] - \bar x}{\sigma} \right)^4 - 3$$
$\qquad \quad$where the $−3$ term makes the value zero for a normal distribution.

    • 


$$\frac{x_{\max}}{x_{\text{rms}}}$$
    

  • Impulse factor
    • 


$$\frac{x_{\max}}{x_{\text{mean}}}$$
    

  • Shape factor
    • 


$$\frac{x_{\text{rms}}}{x_{\text{mean}}}$$

2.2. Feature Extraction from Frequency Domain¶
Ampliutes at the specific frequencies


    

  • Harmonics/subharmonics ($1X,\;2X,\;3X, \cdots$)
    • 



    

  • BPFI (ball pass frequency inner race)
    • 


$$\text{BPFI} = \frac{N}{2} \times F \times \left( 1 + \frac{B}{P} \cos \theta \right)$$
    

  • BPFO (ball pass frequency outer race)
    • 


$$\text{BPFO} = \frac{N}{2} \times F \times \left( 1 - \frac{B}{P} \cos \theta \right)$$
    

  • BSF (ball spin frequency)
    • 


$$\text{BSF} = \frac{P}{2B} \times F \times \left[ 1 - \left( \frac{B}{P} \cos \theta \right)^2 \right]$$
    

  • BDF (ball defect frequency)
    • 


$$\text{BDF} = 2 \times \text{BSF} = \frac{P}{B} \times F \times \left[ 1 - \left( \frac{B}{P} \cos \theta \right)^2 \right]$$
